Frequently Asked Questions

Delivery:
Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Standard delivery typically takes 3–5 business days.

Orders:
Q: Can I cancel my order?
A: Orders can be canceled within 24 hours of placement.

Warranty:
Q: Do products include warranty?
A: Most products include a 1-year manufacturer warranty.

Returns:
Q: How do I initiate a return?
A: Contact customer support and follow the return process.

Payments:
Q: What payment methods are supported?
A: Credit cards, debit cards, and digital payment methods are supported.

Shipping:
Q: Can I track my order?
A: Yes, tracking information is provided after shipment.

General:
Q: Are there hidden charges?
A: No, pricing is transparent except for applicable taxes and shipping fees.
