Return and Refund Policy

Overview:
Customers are eligible to return products within 30 days from the date of purchase. This policy ensures that customers
have sufficient time to evaluate products while maintaining operational fairness.

Eligibility Criteria:
- Products must be unused and undamaged
- Original packaging must be intact
- Proof of purchase is required

Return Process:
1. Initiate a return request through customer support
2. Receive approval and return instructions
3. Ship the product back within the specified window

Inspection:
Returned items undergo inspection to verify compliance with policy conditions. Products that fail inspection may be rejected.

Refund Processing:
Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after successful inspection. Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

Exceptions:
Certain items are non-returnable, including:
- Personal care products
- Customized or made-to-order items

Additional Notes:
Return shipping costs are typically borne by the customer unless the return is due to a defect or error.
