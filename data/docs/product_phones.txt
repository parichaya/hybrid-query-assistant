Product Catalog: Smartphones

Nova X Smartphone:
The Nova X Smartphone features a 6.5-inch display, 5G connectivity, and a 48MP camera system.
It is optimized for high-speed data access, multimedia consumption, and mobile gaming.

Performance:
The device provides smooth application switching and handles moderate multitasking effectively.
It performs well in scenarios such as video streaming, social media usage, and gaming.

Camera Capabilities:
The multi-lens camera system allows for detailed photography in various lighting conditions,
including low-light and HDR environments.

Use Cases:

- Basic applications
