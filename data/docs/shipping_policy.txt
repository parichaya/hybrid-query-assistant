Shipping Policy

Order Processing:
Orders are processed within 1–2 business days after confirmation. Processing time may vary during peak periods.

Shipping Options:
- Standard Shipping: 3–5 business days
- Express Shipping: 1–2 business days

Tracking:
Customers receive a tracking ID after shipment, enabling real-time monitoring of delivery status.

International Shipping:
Available for select regions. Customers are responsible for applicable customs duties and taxes.

Delivery Constraints:
Delivery timelines are estimates and may vary due to:
- Weather conditions
- Logistics disruptions
- Regional accessibility

Packaging:
Products are packaged to ensure safety during transit. Improper handling during return shipping may affect eligibility for refunds.
