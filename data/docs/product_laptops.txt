Product Catalog: Laptops

Acme Pro Laptop:
The Acme Pro Laptop is designed for professionals who require consistent and reliable performance across demanding workloads.
It features a 14-inch full HD display, 16GB RAM, and a 512GB SSD. This configuration enables smooth multitasking, fast application loading,
and efficient handling of development environments such as containerized systems and virtual machines.

In real-world scenarios, this laptop performs well under sustained CPU-intensive tasks such as data preprocessing, backend development,
and analytics workflows. Thermal design ensures stable performance even under extended load conditions, preventing throttling and performance drops.

Use Cases:
- Software development (Python, C++, container workflows)
- Data analysis and visualization
- Light machine learning experimentation

Limitations:
While highly capable, this device is not optimized for GPU-heavy workloads such as deep learning training or high-end gaming.

---

Zenith Ultra Laptop:
The Zenith Ultra Laptop is a mid-range device optimized for general productivity. It includes a 15.6-inch display, 8GB RAM,
and a 256GB SSD. It is designed for students and professionals who primarily work with documents, web applications, and communication tools.

Its strength lies in energy efficiency and simplicity. The system remains responsive for standard workloads and is particularly suited
for environments where portability and battery life are prioritized over raw performance.

Use Cases:
- Office productivity (documents, spreadsheets)
- Web-based applications
- Media consumption

---

Orion Gaming Laptop:
The Orion Gaming Laptop is designed for high-performance workloads. It includes a dedicated GPU, advanced cooling systems,
and a high refresh rate display. This device is capable of handling rendering workloads, simulation environments, and modern gaming engines.

It is particularly useful for:
- 3D rendering and modeling
- Simulation and visualization tasks
- GPU-accelerated computation

Trade-offs include higher power consumption and reduced battery life compared to lighter devices.
